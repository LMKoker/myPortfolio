About Me

It sort of feels like I grew up with the internet. My father worked with computers so I had access at a fairly young age. As more features became available I would learn as much proficiency on them as I could so using a computer became as basic a skill to me as reading and writing Around high school is when I figured out how much I could truly do online. My other hobbies of creating music and media was all possible through programs like Musescore, Windows Movie Maker and Audacity. I took a computer science course in high school and that opened up my horizons even further With some college courses, bootcamps and online courses I learned the basics of languages like C+, Python and Delphi and I am confident in my abilities to take this prerequisite knowledge and apply it to any new language I need. In my free time I lead and am apart of several organized groups of people who come together for collaborative storytelling. I have a love for playing sports more than watching them but I've got some die hard teams I support.
